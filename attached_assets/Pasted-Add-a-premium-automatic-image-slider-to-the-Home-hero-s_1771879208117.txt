Add a premium, automatic image slider to the Home (hero) section.

GOAL:
Make the homepage feel modern, professional, and premium using a smooth, unstoppable image slider that matches the brand UI and colors.

-----------------------------------
SLIDER REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------------
- Automatically slides continuously (no pause, no stop)
- No arrows, no dots, no manual controls
- Smooth fade or slow slide transition
- Infinite loop
- Performance optimized
- Mobile responsive

-----------------------------------
IMAGES
-----------------------------------
Use 10 high-quality images related to:
- House painting
- Building painting
- Spray painting
- High-rise spider work
- Glass cleaning
- Professional painters at work

Images should:
- Match yellow / pink / white brand palette
- Look professional (not stocky or cheap)
- Be optimized (lazy-loaded, compressed)
- Stored in /public/images/hero/

-----------------------------------
DESIGN & UX
-----------------------------------
- Slider should act as a visual background or right-side visual
- Apply a subtle dark or brand-colored overlay for text readability
- Hero text must remain clear and readable on all images
- No text jumping during slide change

-----------------------------------
ANIMATION
-----------------------------------
- Use Framer Motion OR lightweight CSS animation
- Transition duration: slow and elegant
- No flashy effects
- Respect reduced-motion preferences

-----------------------------------
CONTENT OVERLAY
-----------------------------------
Overlay the slider with:
- H1: “Professional Painting Services in Ammandivilai”
- Subtext: “Painting & Building Maintenance Services”
- Two CTA buttons:
  [ Call Now ]
  [ WhatsApp Now ]

Buttons must remain fixed while images slide.

-----------------------------------
PERFORMANCE RULES
-----------------------------------
- Do NOT block page load
- Use optimized images
- Avoid heavy JS libraries
- Mobile-first behavior

-----------------------------------
FINAL GOAL
-----------------------------------
The hero section should feel:
- Premium
- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Professional

It should look like a high-end local service business website, not a template or AI demo.
